About Our Server

Welcome to TheContaminatedZone, a unique Minecraft box server experience where your survival skills will be put to the ultimate test. Players must rely on their own resourcefulness and collaboration to thrive in our challenging environment.

Our server is built on the philosophy of box styled gens.

Server Features

Box Server

Experience Minecraft in a box-based gens.

PvP Enabled

Test your combat skills against other players in the box and maybe special events.

Custom Plugins

Enjoy a variety of custom plugins that enhance gameplay while maintaining the core Minecraft experience.

Active Community

Join our friendly and active community of players from around the world.

Regular Events

Participate in server-wide events with unique challenges and valuable rewards.

Dedicated Staff

Our experienced staff team is always ready to help and ensure a fair gaming experience for everyone.

Server Rules

  1. No Griefing: Respect other players' builds and property. Destruction of other players' creations is strictly prohibited.
  2. No Hacking/Cheating: Use of hacked clients, X-ray texture packs, or any mod that gives an unfair advantage is not allowed.
  3. Be Respectful: Treat all players with respect. Harassment, bullying, or excessive toxicity will not be tolerated.
  4. No Spamming: Avoid spamming chat with repeated messages or excessive caps.
  5. English Only: Please use English in public chat to ensure everyone can understand.
  6. No Advertising: Promoting other servers or services is not allowed.
  7. Follow Staff Instructions: Listen to and comply with staff directions at all times.
  8. Report Issues: Report bugs, glitches, or rule-breakers to staff rather than exploiting them.
